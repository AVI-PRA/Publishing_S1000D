General

S1000D is an international specification for the procurement and production of technical publications. It is an XML Extensible Markup Language specification for preparing, managing, and using equipment maintenance and operations information. It was initially developed by the ASD AeroSpace Defence Industries Association of Europe for use with military aircraft Any fixed-wing or rotary-wing aircraft that is operated by a legal or insurrectionary armed service of any type . The specification has since been modified for use with land, sea, and commercial equipment. S1000D is part of the S-Series of ILS specifications.

S1000D is maintained by the S1000D Steering Committee, which includes board members from ASD, the United States' AIA Aerospace Industries Association , and the ATA Air Transport Association , along with national industry and defence representatives from most of the countries currently using the specification.
